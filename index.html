📖 Story: The Power of Determination

Ravi was a young boy from a small village who dreamed of becoming a successful engineer. His family was very poor, and they struggled to meet their daily needs. Despite these difficulties, Ravi was determined to achieve his goal. Every day, he walked nearly five kilometers to reach his school because there was no transportation available. After returning home, Ravi helped his parents with household work and farming. Even though he was tired, he never skipped his studies. He studied late into the night using a small lamp. Many people in his village laughed at him and said that he would never succeed in life. However, Ravi ignored their negative words and stayed focused on his dream. Ravi used books from the local library and also learned from free online videos whenever he got access to the internet. Slowly, his understanding improved, and he started scoring high marks in his exams. His teachers noticed his dedication and supported him in every possible way. One day, Ravi got an opportunity to appear for a national-level scholarship exam. He prepared day and night without wasting a single moment. When the results were announced, Ravi secured the first rank. This achievement changed his life completely. With the help of the scholarship, Ravi joined a reputed engineering college. His journey inspired many students in his village. Ravi proved that determination, hard work, and belief in oneself can overcome even the toughest challenges in life.
